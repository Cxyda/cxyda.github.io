I'm Daniel game developer by heart and soul. Nice to e-meet you! I started my professional career in 2009 as a 3D Artist working at a small company which did PC retail titles. After that I worked in the browser and mobile market industry for Bigpoint and Innogames in Hamburg Germany.

After 8 years of working as a 3D Artist and reaching the senior level I decided to leave my comfort zone and take a new path to do the thing what I love to do in my spare time as a full time job: Programming games in Unity3D! So I started to study software engineering in my spare time besides my regular full-time job as a senior 3D artist and finished my bachelors degree (B.Sc.) 3.5 years later.

In 01/2018 Innogames gave me the trust and opportunity to switch my contract to a full-time software developer. Since then, I'm working on mobile titles created with Unity3D.

In my spare time I like to work on my own games which are mostly strategy heavy, but so far, I was never able to complete one before I started 'the next big thing'. Also, I like to do sports, gaming, watch movies and hang out with friends.

In my blog you will find projects and tutorials I'm working on mostly related to C# and Unity3D. If you have questions, comments or just want to say 'hi' feel free to send me a message.
